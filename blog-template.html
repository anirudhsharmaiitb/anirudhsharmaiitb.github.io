Understanding AI Basics

Published on Aug 10, 2025 · 5 min read

Artificial Intelligence (AI) has become one of the most influential technologies of the 21st century. From recommendation systems on Netflix to self-driving cars, AI is everywhere.

What is AI?

At its core, AI is about making machines mimic human intelligence — learning, reasoning, and problem-solving.

"AI is not about replacing humans. It is about augmenting human capabilities."

Example: A Simple Python Program


def greet(name):
    return f"Hello, {name}! Welcome to AI world."

print(greet("Anirudh"))

The above code is a simple demonstration, but in practice AI models involve complex algorithms, data, and computing power.

Conclusion

AI is rapidly evolving and will continue to shape industries. Understanding its basics helps us appreciate its power and prepare for its future impact.
